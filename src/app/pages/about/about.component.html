About this App

This app showcases Angular Material and demonstrates various features, including data management with NGRX, dynamic theme switching, and form validation. The app is divided into three main pages:

Home: A table with filtering and sorting capabilities to easily manage data.
Books: A grid displaying books stored in the NGRX store, with options to create, update, or delete entries. The form for creating/updating books uses form validation within an expansion panel.
About: Change the app's theme with a single click and switch between light/dark mode for a personalized experience.

Enjoy exploring the app and customizing its look and functionality to fit your needs!
